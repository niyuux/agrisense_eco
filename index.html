About Agrisense

Agrisense is a non-profit organization dedicated to revolutionizing agricultural practices through innovative technology. We focus on creating sustainable solutions that address food security challenges while minimizing environmental impact.

Founded in 2020, our team brings together experts in agriculture, engineering, and community development with a shared vision of making advanced farming techniques accessible to all communities.

Our Journey

Agrisense began with a simple question: How can we grow more nutritious food with fewer resources? After years of research and development, we created the Malunggay Aeroponic System—a breakthrough technology that enables efficient cultivation of moringa, one of the most nutrient-dense plants on the planet.

Our systems have been deployed in various communities across the Philippines, helping to address malnutrition while providing economic opportunities for local farmers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Malunggay Aeroponic System.

What is an aeroponic system?

Aeroponics is a plant cultivation technique where roots are suspended in air and periodically misted with nutrient-rich water. This technique provides optimal oxygen exposure to roots, resulting in faster growth and higher nutrient content compared to traditional soil farming.

Why focus on Malunggay (Moringa)?

Malunggay is one of the most nutrient-dense plants on the planet, containing high levels of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and protein. It's drought-resistant and fast-growing, making it an ideal crop for addressing nutritional challenges in communities worldwide.

How does the mobile application work?

Our mobile application connects to your aeroponic system via WiFi and provides real-time monitoring of environmental conditions (temperature, humidity, nutrient levels, pH). It also allows remote control of system functions and sends alerts when parameters are outside optimal ranges.

What is the ARIMA forecasting model?

ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average) is a statistical model we implement to predict future growth patterns based on historical data from your system. This helps optimize nutrient delivery, harvest timing, and overall yield by making data-driven predictions about plant development.

How much space do I need for the system?

Our standard Malunggay Aeroponic System has a footprint of approximately 120cm x 60cm and a height of 180cm. We recommend allowing at least 30cm of clearance around all sides for easy access and maintenance.

What is the return on investment?

Most users see a complete return on investment within 12-18 months through savings on water, fertilizer, and increased yield. The system produces approximately 20-30% more harvest compared to traditional growing methods.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, our team provides professional installation and initial setup services. This includes system calibration, mobile app setup, and hands-on training for your team.

How long does the system last?

With proper maintenance, our aeroponic systems have an expected lifespan of 7-10 years. The main pump and electrical components are designed for 5+ years of continuous operation.

Maintenance Tips

Follow these guidelines to keep your Malunggay Aeroponic System running optimally for years to come.

Daily Maintenance

  • Check water levels in the reservoir
  • Monitor system temperature (optimal range: 22-28°C)
  • Inspect plants for any signs of stress
  • Review mobile app notifications and alerts

Weekly Maintenance

  • Test and adjust pH levels (target: 5.8-6.2)
  • Check EC/TDS of nutrient solution
  • Clean any debris from growing chambers
  • Inspect misting nozzles for clogs
  • Prune plants as needed

Monthly Maintenance

  • Complete water change and system flush
  • Clean filters and replace if necessary
  • Sanitize all growing surfaces
  • Check pump pressure and operation
  • Inspect all electrical connections
  • Update mobile app and firmware if available

Quarterly Maintenance

  • Deep clean the entire system
  • Replace misting nozzles
  • Check system calibration
  • Evaluate ARIMA model predictions against actual results
  • Backup system data from mobile application

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Poor Plant Growth

Check nutrient solution concentration, pH levels, and ensure misting cycles are functioning properly. Review historical data in the mobile app to identify potential patterns.

System Not Misting Properly

Inspect nozzles for clogs, check pump pressure, and verify timer settings. Clean or replace nozzles as needed.

Mobile App Connection Issues

Ensure your WiFi connection is stable, restart the system controller, and verify that your mobile device has the latest app version installed.

Unusual Noise from Pump

Check for debris in the pump, inspect water levels, and verify that the pump is properly mounted to reduce vibration.
